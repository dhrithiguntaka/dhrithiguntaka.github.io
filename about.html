About Us

Discover our story and the flavors we bring to life.

Amorantha combines the Spanish word for love, Amor, and the name of a staple grain in Indian cuisine, Amaranth.

This playful mix of words reflects our restaurant's Indian and Spanish fusion cuisine and the love we put into our dishes.

Our restaurant's colors are yellow, muave, and green. They embody the vibrancy of our meals and the richness of the cultures they represent. These colors are recognized in the many meals of those countries as well- mauve can evoke a sense of elegance and sophistication, blending both cultures' rich aesthetics; rice and spice usage represents yellow; and the abundance of plant-based meals expresses the green portion.
