Our Process

Cultivating our Fresh Ingredients

At Amorantha, we believe in bringing the freshest ingredients directly to your table by cultivating them on our very own on-site farm. Under the expertise of our wonderful farmers, Robert Glinsky and Jake Thompson, we grow a wide range of seasonal produce using organic methods that ensure the highest quality. From leafy greens and vibrant vegetables to fragrant herbs, every crop is nurtured with care. Both Robert and Jake are committed to sustainable farming practices, using no pesticides and promoting healthy soil through composting and crop rotation. Our manager, Rajiv Kapoor, ensures that all of this aligns with our restaurant’s commitment to sustainability, overseeing the smooth integration of farm-fresh ingredients into the kitchen.

Harvesting for the Season's Best Flavors

Once the crops are ready, Robert and Jake collaborate closely with our head chef Lorena Martinez to ensure that everything is harvested at its peak. With fresh, seasonal ingredients like juicy tomatoes and aromatic herbs, each dish celebrates the natural flavors of the farm. Because we grow our produce on-site, it’s often harvested just hours before it reaches your plate, ensuring exceptional freshness. Chef Martinez carefully selects the best ingredients to craft dishes that highlight the unique flavors of each season, creating an authentic and unique dining experience that celebrates the earth’s bounty.

From Field to Kitchen and back

Our farm-to-table process doesn’t stop at harvest. Every food scrap from our kitchen is carefully composted to nurture the next season's crops. With the help of Robert and Jake, we ensure that all organic waste is put back into the farm’s ecosystem, creating a system that minimizes waste and maximizes sustainability. This process supports the farm, reduces our carbon footprint, and ensures that we’re continuously improving the health of our land. Manager Kapoor is very efficient in making sure these zero-waste practices are fully implemented, and that Amorantha stays true to its environmental commitment.

Bringing the Farm to Your Plate

As soon as the harvest is complete, our extremely talented chefs transform the produce into vibrant dishes that showcase the best of what the farm has to offer. Under the guidance of our head chef, our menu evolves based on the season's harvest. By growing the ingredients ourselves and working alongside our amazing farmers, we ensure that our dishes are made with the freshest, most flavorful ingredients and most importantly love. By placing sustainability as our top priority, we offer a dining experience that is not only exceptional in flavor but is also in harmony with the Earth, fostering a healthy lifestyle with every bite.

Meet the Team

At Amorantha, we take pride in the hard work our chefs and farmers put into each and every meal. They work together to create each dish with love and consideration for the environment. Our staff makes sure that every plate is a tribute to sustainability and quality by cultivating and harvesting fresh, organic foods every day on our on-site farm.
